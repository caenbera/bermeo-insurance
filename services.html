Our Methodology

The Bermeo Process

A structured and rigorous path to ensure excellence at every stage of service.

1

Discovery and Diagnosis

We meet privately to analyze your current situation in detail: assets, family commitments, corporate structures, personal goals, and risk tolerance. This diagnosis forms the foundation of your plan.

2

Bespoke Strategy Design

Our team of analysts and planners models quantitative scenarios and designs a custom strategy. We propose optimal insurance coverages, trust structures, and portfolio allocations for your review.

3

Implementation and Coordination

Once the strategy is approved, we proceed to bind high-limit policies, coordinate details with your legal and tax advisors, and allocate capital to selected investment portfolios.

4

Active Monitoring & Review

The macroeconomic environment and your life change. We constantly monitor strategy performance and hold periodic review meetings to rebalance portfolios and adjust coverages as needed.
